Synopsis Rebirth of the Divine Demon

A legendary being who wished to sweep the Jianghu in a storm of blood, the Divine Demon, Jeong Cheon.

At the mere mention of his name, murim trembled in fear, and his sword split the very heavens.

Even the elite warriors of the Nine Great Sects, none could stand as his equal.

His martial prowess that shook the world was passed down as a myth, yet in the end, defying all expectations, he met death. For a brief moment, peace returned to the jianghu, and the tale of the Divine Demon seemed buried within legend.

“But fate did not let him go.”

Jeong Cheon, the Divine Demon, returned to life, However, this time, not as the great warrior as he was in his past life, but in the frail body of a fallen noble's heir, a weak young master plagued by madness.

The soul that once commanded all under heaven is now trapped within a feeble body and endless torment.

The downfall of his clan, the contempt of those around him, and the creeping shadow of insanity devouring his mind, all of it weighs upon his new life.
